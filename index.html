A magazine for the wet and wonderfully scandalous world of FLINTA+

To hold Spit Take in your hands is to feel In with a capital I. In on a secret, a friend group, a conversation, a joke. Each issue of Spit Take begins with a theme and follows a reliable and recognisable order.

Much like a newspaper, Spit Take is broken into familiar sections: sports pages, reviews of the latest queer film or novel, personal ads, interviews with queer women and non-binary people doing extraordinary things, etc.
